Privacy Policy for Ace in the Hole

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Ace in the Hole is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from our users, including:

  • No email addresses
  • No names or personal identifiers
  • No usage analytics or tracking data
  • No user accounts or registration required

2. File Storage

All files you store using Ace in the Hole are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Saved in a secure local folder on your computer
  • Never uploaded to external servers or cloud services
  • Completely under your control and ownership

3. File Sharing Feature

When you use the file sharing functionality:

  • Files are shared directly from your device via your local network (WiFi)
  • Files are transmitted using standard HTTP/FTP protocols
  • No files are uploaded to or stored on external servers
  • Sharing only occurs when you actively initiate it
  • Other users can access shared files only when you provide them with the QR code or IP address

4. Developer Access

We (the app developers) cannot and do not:

  • Access your files or data
  • View what files you store
  • Monitor your app usage
  • Collect any information about your activities

5. Third-Party Services

Ace in the Hole does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No external APIs or cloud services

6. Data Security

Your data security is maintained through:

  • Local storage only - files never leave your device except when you share them
  • Secure folder location that is not easily accessible to other applications
  • No transmission of data to external servers

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, this policy applies to all age groups.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any personal data about you:

  • There is no personal data to delete or modify
  • You have complete control over your files on your device
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all associated data
